Lesson 2: Strings and Chords

In this lesson, we will have introduced the different strings and notes on the guitar, from the smallest string to the largest string: EBGDAE. We will go over different playing styles and techniques. We will also learn 3 new chords today, C, G, D. With these 3 chords, you can now play hundreds of songs. The following song “We’re Gonna Be Friends” is a popular one from the early 2000’s, played with a finger plucking style.
